# Engagement optimizes against everything that matters

The algorithm is unbiased in the worst way — it just prefers whatever keeps you scrolling. Outrage, fear, and tribalism win the attention competition because they trigger identity-level responses. The feed replaces boredom, connection, and rest with engagement-optimized substitutes that look like what they replaced.
