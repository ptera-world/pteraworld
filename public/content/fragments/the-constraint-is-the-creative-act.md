# The constraint is the creative act

a blank canvas with no requirements, no deadline, no audience, no medium constraint — and nothing happens. the creative act isn't working within constraints, it's choosing them. the decision to use only these colors, to write in this form, to solve this problem and not that one. every constraint narrows the space and simultaneously makes something visible that wasn't before. the freedom is in the choosing, not in the having-more-options.
