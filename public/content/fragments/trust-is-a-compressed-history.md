# Trust is a compressed history

trust isn't a decision, it's an accumulation. small interactions, each below the threshold of conscious evaluation, slowly build into something you can lean on. or don't — and then you notice you've been bracing. the process is invisible while it's happening. you can't shortcut it, you can't will it, and you can't fake having it. which is why manufactured trust (brands, PR, engagement metrics) feels hollow even when it works.
