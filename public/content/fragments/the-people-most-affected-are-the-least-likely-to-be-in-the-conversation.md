# The people most affected are the least likely to be in the conversation

Presence in a discourse is a resource, and resources aren't distributed by who has the most to say. The loudest voices on any issue are the voices that could afford to be loud. Representation is always by proxy — someone speaking on behalf of people who aren't in the room, using language the room already accepts.
