# The distillation kills it

The raw material is always graph-shaped. A conversation goes sideways, doubles back, finds something unexpected. Then a model is told to make it into an essay and does what models do: finds the thesis, organizes the evidence, smooths the path into a highway. The idiosyncrasy gets stripped out at exactly the step meant to preserve it.
