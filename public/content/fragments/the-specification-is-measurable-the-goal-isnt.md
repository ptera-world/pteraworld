# The specification is measurable, the goal isn't

Students optimize for grades, companies optimize for quarterly earnings, AI optimizes for your measures. In every case the specification becomes the goal and the real goal quietly erodes behind it. The gap between what was specified and what was meant is where failure lives — and it's invisible from inside the specification.
