# Good work is gated behind wealth, not skill

The intersection of skills needed for excellent work is vanishingly rare, but it exists. What's scarce is the freedom to sit at that intersection full-time without starving. Good software, good art, good anything — the price is time, and time is only free if you've already solved money.
