# The opposite of control isn't chaos

tighten every parameter and the system does exactly what you specified, which is never quite what you wanted. loosen the constraints and things self-organize in ways you didn't predict — and often couldn't have designed. the fear is that less control means more chaos, but what usually happens is a different kind of order. one that fits the actual situation instead of the plan.
