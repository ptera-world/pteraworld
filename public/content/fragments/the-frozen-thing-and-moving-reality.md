# The frozen thing and moving reality create the gap that breaks things

People freeze identity into labels. Software freezes paradigms. Institutions freeze culture. Reality keeps moving. The gap between the frozen thing and the moving reality grows until something breaks. Impermanence isn't the problem — the refusal to let things change is.
