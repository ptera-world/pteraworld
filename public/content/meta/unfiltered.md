same map, different voice. the essays here say what the polished versions point at but don't say.

## what is this?

a parallel view of the same graph. the projects, the connections, the spatial arrangement — all identical. what changes is the writing.

the main site's essays use calculated compassion — they describe situations without negativity because the page doesn't know who's reading. someone in a fine headspace gets outrage, someone already not OK gets despair. the restraint is deliberate.

these essays drop the restraint.
