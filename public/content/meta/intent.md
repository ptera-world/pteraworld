a map of essays about intent — what it was, what it did, and what happens to things that depended on it.

## what is this?

somewhere between art criticism and systems thinking. the essays here start with specific complaints — about AI, about algorithms, about how recommendations work — and follow them back to the same place: the assumption that output signals intent, and what happens when that assumption breaks.

the concern isn't new. the blindness to it mostly is.
